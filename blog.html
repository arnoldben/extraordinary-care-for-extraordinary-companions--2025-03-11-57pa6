The Complete Guide to Pet Dental Health: Everything You Need to Know

Maintaining your pet's dental health is essential for their overall well-being, yet it's often overlooked. Poor dental hygiene can lead to serious health issues affecting the heart, liver, and kidneys. A comprehensive dental care routine includes daily brushing with pet-specific toothpaste, dental wipes, chews, and toys. Watch for warning signs like bad breath, yellow teeth, and swollen gums. Professional veterinary cleanings are crucial and should include examinations, X-rays, and scaling. Choose VOHC-approved products and maintain a tooth-friendly diet. Prevention is key – starting early with proper dental care can save costly procedures and protect your pet's health.
